1. Introduction

MegaMart is committed to environmental responsibility and sustainable business practices. This E-waste Policy outlines our approach to managing electronic waste (e-waste) in compliance with applicable laws and regulations, particularly the E-Waste (Management) Rules, 2016 and subsequent amendments in India.

2. What is E-waste?

Electronic waste or e-waste refers to discarded electrical or electronic devices. This includes:

  • Smartphones, tablets, and mobile accessories
  • Laptops, computers, and peripherals
  • Television sets and audio equipment
  • Home appliances (refrigerators, washing machines, microwaves)
  • Gaming consoles and electronic toys
  • Batteries and chargers
  • LED bulbs and electronic lighting

3. Our Environmental Commitment

Reduce

We promote the purchase of energy-efficient and durable electronic products to minimize waste generation.

Reuse

We encourage customers to donate or sell working electronics before disposing of them.

Recycle

We facilitate proper recycling of electronic waste through authorized recycling partners.

Responsible

We ensure all e-waste is handled by certified recyclers following environmental standards.

4. Customer Responsibilities

4.1 Before Disposal

  • Remove all personal data from devices
  • Remove batteries from devices where possible
  • Keep original accessories and packaging when disposing
  • Check if the device can be repaired instead of replaced

4.2 Proper Disposal

Do not dispose of electronic items in regular household waste. Use authorized e-waste collection centers or recycling programs to ensure proper handling.

5. E-waste Collection Program

5.1 Take-Back Program

MegaMart offers a take-back program for customers purchasing new electronic items. When you buy eligible products, you can return your old electronics of the same category for proper disposal.

5.2 Collection Points

We have partnered with authorized e-waste collection centers in major cities. Visit our website or contact customer service to find the nearest collection point.

5.3 Free Collection Service

For bulk e-waste (more than 5 items), we offer free doorstep collection service in select cities. Schedule a pickup through our customer service.

6. Authorized Recycling Partners

We work exclusively with certified e-waste recyclers who comply with:

  • Central Pollution Control Board (CPCB) authorization
  • State Pollution Control Board (SPCB) permissions
  • ISO 14001 Environmental Management Standards
  • Responsible recycling practices
  • Data destruction certifications
